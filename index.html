Field Notes

Dance//Novella Arts Society is the only professional dance company based in North Vancouver, BC. Our program brings high-quality contemporary dance to a community that rarely experiences live professional performances. In addition to live shows, we offer community workshops that further engage and enrich our audience. For the wider community beyond the North Shore, our program reflects the diversity of BC, allowing community members to connect through our diverse representation.
